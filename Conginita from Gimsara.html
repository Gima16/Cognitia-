About Gimsara C. Jayarathne

Welcome to Conginita. I am Gimsara C. Jayarathne, a committed psychology student and counselor dedicated to providing expert psychological support and guidance. Currently pursuing a BSc in Psychology at NSBM Green University, I am passionate about helping individuals achieve mental well-being through personalized and empathetic counseling.

With a solid foundation in psychological theory and practice, I am equipped to offer a range of counseling services designed to support your personal development and well-being. My approach is grounded in client-centered therapy, focusing on understanding your individual experiences and working collaboratively to achieve your therapeutic goals.

Our Services

Individual Counseling

Personalized one-on-one sessions focusing on your unique mental health challenges and goals. Our sessions are designed to offer a safe and supportive environment for self-exploration and growth.

Couples Therapy

Guided sessions aimed at improving communication and resolving conflicts within relationships. We work together to strengthen your bond and foster a healthier partnership.

Group Therapy

Facilitated group sessions that provide support through shared experiences. This format helps build a sense of community and mutual understanding among participants.

Workshops and Seminars

Interactive sessions on various topics related to mental health and personal development. Our workshops are designed to provide valuable insights and practical tools for managing life’s challenges.
